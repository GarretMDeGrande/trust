Welcome to Trust Clinic

Trust Clinic is a 501(c)3 nonprofit Mental Healthcare organization in Washington State.

Therapy sessions are completely Telehealth.

Our Mission

Our mission at Trust Clinic is to provide affordable therapy services to vulnerable individuals in our communities. We are committed to offering low-rate sessions to those in need, while actively seeking donations from individuals and organizations to sustain this initiative.
